Code of Conduct – Cybermeister CTF

By participating in this Capture The Flag (CTF) event, you agree to the following rules. This platform is intended for learning, fair play, and technical challenge.

  • Fair play: Exploiting vulnerabilities outside the intended scope of a challenge is not allowed. Attacking the CTF platform itself, scoreboard, authentication mechanisms, or infrastructure is prohibited.
  • Scope & isolation: Only interact with systems and services that are explicitly part of the challenge. Accessing other teams’ environments or bypassing isolation is forbidden.
  • Flags & sharing: Flags are confidential and may not be shared between teams. Automated flag submission or brute-forcing flags is not allowed.
  • Conduct: Respect other participants and organizers. Harassment, discrimination, or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Accounts: Each participant may only use one account. Account sharing or impersonation is not allowed.
  • Monitoring & enforcement: Platform activity may be logged and monitored. The organizers may reset challenges, adjust scores, or disqualify participants if necessary.
  • Educational use: Techniques learned during this CTF may only be used on systems you own or have explicit permission to test.

By continuing, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to these rules.
